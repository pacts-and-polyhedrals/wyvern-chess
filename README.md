## What is Wyvern Chess?

Wyvern Chess is a fantasy strategy game played across three stacked boards: the Sky, the Land, and the Underworld.

It takes the familiar feel of chess and turns it into a layered tavern game of dragons, griffons, clerics, warriors, basilisk freezes, underworld eruptions, and skyborne threats.

Play against Grukk the Goblin, challenge a friend in Pass & Play, or learn the rules through a full interactive lesson course.

---

## ✨ Features

### Player Tools

🧑‍🏫 **Lessons with Grukk** — learn every piece, tactic, and board layer through guided puzzles  
⚔️ **Quick Match vs Grukk** — play a full game against the goblin AI  
🔥 **Warlord Mode** — face a stronger, deeper-thinking version of Grukk  
👥 **Pass & Play** — two players on one device  
🛡 **Teams Mode** — name the Gold and Scarlet sides for table play  
🎨 **Sprite Art** — fantasy piece art for both armies  
📜 **Move Log** — track every move and capture  
💀 **Captured Tray** — see which pieces have fallen  
💡 **Hint System** — get a suggested move when you need a nudge  
🧭 **Tilt / Flatten Board View** — switch between angled and flat board layouts  

### Game Pieces

🧚 **Sylphs** — sky pawns that drift and dive  
🦅 **Griffons** — long-leaping sky hunters  
🐉 **Dragons** — aerial lords that breathe fire onto the Land  
⚔️ **Warriors** — land pawns that promote into Heroes  
🐘 **Oliphants** — straight-line chargers  
🦄 **Unicorns** — leaping fork pieces  
🛡 **Heroes** — diagonal champions that climb between boards  
🗡 **Thieves** — long diagonal attackers  
✨ **Clerics** — flexible support pieces that move across levels  
🧙 **Mages** — queen-like land masters with vertical magic  
⚜️ **Paladins** — elite champions with powerful vaulting movement  
👑 **Kings** — vulnerable monarchs who can flee between boards  
⛏ **Dwarves** — underworld miners that strike upward  
🐍 **Basilisks** — freezing pieces that lock enemies in place  
🔥 **Elementals** — underworld forces that erupt onto the Land

## How to Play

Wyvern Chess uses three 12x8 boards:

**Sky** — flying pieces and aerial threats  
**Land** — the main battlefield  
**Underworld** — hidden pressure, freezes, and eruptions  

Gold moves first. Checkmate the enemy King to win.

New players should start with **Lessons with Grukk**, which teaches the pieces first, then tactics, then full-board strategy.
